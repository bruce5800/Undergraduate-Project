\begin{abstract}
\textbf{中文摘要。}大语言模型（LLM）推理负载的快速增长，给数据中心带来巨大的服务成本与能耗压力，仅以吞吐量为单一优化目标的传统调度器已难以胜任。本文研究\textbf{云--边协同的 LLM 推理调度问题}，并显式建模五项 AIGC 特有的物理约束：模型权重驻留、KV 缓存随 token 增长的显存占用、连续批处理（continuous batching）、解码阶段的显存带宽下限，以及预填充--解码（prefill/decode）两阶段请求生命周期。我们设计了一个面向 AIGC 物理的 PPO 调度器，将上述物理量通过状态特征与奖励整形显式暴露给策略网络，并在涵盖启发式、负载均衡与学习式方法的 8 个强基线上做对比。在覆盖拓扑、负载与工作负载组合的 30 种配置（每种 $N=30$ 次独立运行）下，本文调度器于典型工况取得严格的 Pareto 占优：相对最强基线，\slo{} 达成率提升 28\%（统计显著，$p<0.05$），同时每 token 能耗降低 7\%。然而 12 组件消融分析揭示出一个反直觉发现：联合改进是\textbf{系统级整体贡献}而非某项奖励技巧——仅"连续批处理仿真"与"充足预训练"个体显著，所有单项 AIGC 状态或奖励组件在单独消融时与完整模型在统计上不可区分。我们开源仿真器及 30+ 套基准 manifest，以支持后续 AIGC 调度研究。

\textbf{关键词：}LLM 推理；AIGC；云边协同；强化学习；调度；能效；Pareto 优化。

\end{abstract}

% --- English Abstract（中文期刊要求双语并存） -----------------
\noindent\textbf{Abstract.} The rapid growth of large language
model (LLM) inference workloads imposes serving-cost and energy
pressures that single-axis throughput-oriented schedulers cannot
address. We study \emph{cloud--edge collaborative LLM inference
scheduling} under five AIGC-specific physical constraints:
model-weight residency, KV-cache memory growth, continuous
batching, the memory-bandwidth floor of decode, and the
two-phase prefill/decode request lifecycle. We design an
AIGC-aware proximal policy optimization (PPO) scheduler that
exposes these physics to the policy through state features and
reward shaping, and compare against eight strong baselines
spanning heuristic, load-balancing, and learned approaches.
Across 30 configurations sweeping topology, load, and workload
composition ($N{=}30$ runs each), our scheduler achieves strict
Pareto dominance in the canonical operating regime: a 28\% gain
in \slo{} attainment (statistically significant, $p<0.05$)
together with 7\% lower energy per token relative to the
strongest baseline. A 12-component ablation reveals that the
gain is a holistic system contribution: only continuous-batching
simulation and adequate pretraining are individually
significant; every AIGC reward and state component is
statistically indistinguishable from the full model when ablated
alone. We release the simulator and the 30+ benchmark manifests
to support future AIGC scheduling research.

\noindent\textbf{Key words:} LLM inference; AIGC; cloud-edge
computing; reinforcement learning; scheduling; energy
efficiency; Pareto optimization.
